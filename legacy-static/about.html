Our story

A family-run cleaner, one careful order at a time.

We opened our shutters in Idrees Market with one rule: treat every kameez, every coat, every saree like it belongs to our own family. A decade later, it's still the only rule.

Started in F10. Stayed in F10.

New Way began as a single steam press behind the counter at Idrees Market. Word travelled, the queue grew, and one machine became three. Today we look after thousands of orders a month — and we still know most of our customers by name.

Nothing leaves our shop until it has been hand-checked, tagged with your name and packed in a protective cover. Nothing about that has changed since day one — and it never will.

What we stand for

Three things we won't compromise on.

01 · Care

Like it's our own.

If we wouldn't wear it after the clean, you don't get it back. Every garment is checked twice — once on arrival, once before delivery.

02 · Honesty

The price is the price.

No "fancy fabric" surcharges sprung at delivery. Every rate is printed on our services page — no exceptions, no surprises.

03 · Time

When we say a day, we mean a day.

Most orders return in 24 hours. Same-day service is available. You'll get a text when your order is on its way home.
